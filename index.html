Maeve's Creamy Tomato Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 crushed garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • 2 chopped celery sticks
  • 200g chopped carrots
  • 0.5 tbsp sugar
  • 1 large sprig fresh thyme
  • 750g ripe plum tomatos, roughly chopped
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 200ml passata
  • 500ml vegetable stock
  • 100ml single cream or low-fat crème fraîche
  • splash dry sherry
  • crusty bread and butter, to serve

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan and cook the onion for 5–6 minutes over a gentle heat until almost softened but not browned.
  2. Add the garlic, celery, carrots, bay leaf and thyme and cook for a further 6–7 minutes, stirring occasionally, making sure the vegetables don't stick to the base of the pan.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and sugar and season well with salt and pepper. Cook for a few minutes, then stir in the passata and vegetable stock. Bring to a simmer, cover with a lid and cook for 35–40 minutes, until all the vegetables are tender.
  4. Remove the bay leaf and thyme, then blend the soup using a food processor or blender. This will probably have to be done in two batches.
  5. Pour the soup into a clean saucepan (you can pass it through a sieve if you want it really smooth). Stir in the cream, a splash of sherry and extra seasoning if required.
  6. Pour into large mugs or bowls and serve with fresh crusty buttered bread.

Dawud's Pizza Dough Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g bread flour
  • 7g dried yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 325ml warm water

Instructions:

  1. Mix the flour, yeast, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add the water and oil, stirring until the dough forms.
  3. Knead on a floured surface for 10 minutes until smooth and stretchy.
  4. Place in a bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour.
  5. Divide into balls, roll out thin, and add toppings.
  6. Bake at 220°C+ in your oven for 8-12 minutes.
